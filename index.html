Merchants

Why they are the best class

Obviously it sounds like they'd be a pretty bad class, and souly a support class. But they have a lot of potential.

Top three reasons:

  1. They start with a "bag of holding".
  2. The bag of holding can summon items from thin air.
  3. They can barter and get better deals when buying or selling anything.

More reasons:

  • Stuck somewhere? Merchant bag can summon an item to get you out.
  • Need food? Merchant bag can summon food.
  • Need a weapon? Merchant bag.
See? A very handy class.
Now of course you'll need to be in a group with other classes... or do you?
